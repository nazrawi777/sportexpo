Ethiopia's first comprehensive sports industry exhibition

Ethiopia: The Epicenter of Global Athletics

Addis Ababa is no longer just the political capital of Africa; it is the heartbeat of world-class sports. Following the historic success of the 2026 Athletics Grand Prix, Sport Expo ET 2026 brings together pioneers of sports technology, infrastructure, wellness, and performance.

Our vision is to unify the sports economy by bridging cutting-edge global innovation with Ethiopia's untapped potential. The Expo connects local SMEs, federations, clubs, equipment suppliers, health and wellness providers, investors, and international manufacturers in one professional marketplace.

Organized by Adulian Advertising & Promotion PLC and Diamond Marketing & Communication PLC, the event combines large-scale activation experience with contemporary digital engagement and high-end corporate event management.
