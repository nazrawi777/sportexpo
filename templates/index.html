Executive Summary

Uniting Ethiopia Through
Sport, Innovation & Growth

Sport Expo ET 2026 is Ethiopia's first comprehensive sports industry exhibition, hosted in Addis Ababa to unite stakeholders across the national sports ecosystem. The Expo bridges local talent, private industry, and international standards to professionalize the sector, stimulate economic growth, and promote a healthier, more peaceful nation.

The event showcases the complete sports value chain, creating opportunities for business networking, investment, knowledge transfer, innovation, wellness, and sustainable sports development while positioning Ethiopia as a leading regional hub for sports business and tourism.
